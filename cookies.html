Cookie Policy for COZA


1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how COZA uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, customize, evaluate, improve, and secure our services.


2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.


3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for the operation of the website.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites to deliver relevant ads.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices such as language or region.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. You can also use our cookie settings panel to manage preferences. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our site.


5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.


6. Contact Us
