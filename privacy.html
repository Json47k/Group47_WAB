Privacy Policy for COZA


1. Introduction

Welcome to COZA (Creative Outlook of GenZ Attire). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our website, www.coza.com (the "Site").

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any of the terms in this policy, please refrain from using our services.


2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data to enhance your shopping experience. The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, shipping address, billing address, phone number.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and order history.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including browsing behavior, pages visited, and products viewed or purchased.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor and analyze website traffic, behavior, and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for several purposes:

  • To Process Transactions: To process orders, process payments, and deliver products.
  • To Improve Our Services: To improve the functionality of the Site and provide a personalized experience.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you order confirmations, shipping updates, promotions, and customer support messages.
  • To Prevent Fraud: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions and protect our users' accounts.
  • To Provide Marketing and Promotions: We may use your email address to send you promotional emails, discounts, or updates on new arrivals. You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Site, process payments, or fulfill orders.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share your information when required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: If COZA undergoes a business transaction, such as a merger or acquisition, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Security of Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect sensitive data, such as payment information, during transmission. However, no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve the user experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to remember your preferences, track your browsing behavior, and personalize your experience. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some features of the Site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

For more details, you can read our Cookie Policy.


7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we update or correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data in accordance with applicable laws.
  • Opt-out of Marketing Communications: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by following the instructions in the email or contacting us directly.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a commonly used format for transfer to another service provider.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at wab@gmail.com.


8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing them with any personal information.


9. Children's Privacy

COZA does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information as quickly as possible.


10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post any updates on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Please check this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
